What Is Link Building and Why Is It Important?

The definition of link building refers to the process of acquiring links from other websites to your own. The more reputable websites that link to your website, the more Google considers your website an authority in your industry. According to Google, link building remains one of its top three ranking factors. There are different techniques for building links to your website.

A successful link building strategy is one of the most important factors in SEO ranking. A quality link from a highly respected website can lead to a significant increase in referral traffic. If man websites are linking to a page, then the importance of this page will be increased and the page will rank high. A page with more backlinks tends to earn high positions in SERPs.
