What is User Experience (UX) Design?

Professional website design isn’t just about looking good; it’s about providing the end user with a smooth, cohesive journey from landing page to checkout. A well-designed website or product, though, enables a good user experience – one that makes using it effortless and fun. User experience encompasses all aspects of the end-user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products.

UX Design: user experience for your website

User Experience Design is an important part in the phase in which you also work out the functional design of your site. But what exactly is UX design? And what exactly does it add for you?

  • It refers to how they use it – to what ends, by which paths, in what circumstances; which parts or components they take advantage of and which they avoid, neglect, or don’t understand.
  • It refers to the way they feel about the website – is it a chore to use, or a delight? Does it captivate them, does it confuse them, does it frustrate them? Do they feel confident using it?

  • It also includes usability – are users able to achieve their goals on the site? Does the design help or hinder them? Do people understand what they are doing, do they know where to find the things that matter to them and how to navigate the website? Do they know what the icons and labels mean?

Attractive and functional

User-Experience plays a key role in the success of your business website. Understanding the importance of UX for your website is the first step towards creating beautiful, engaging, and useful business solutions. That very initial visual perception can be critical to sparking likeability.

The more engaged the user becomes, the more important usability becomes. The goal of User Experience (UX) design is to make digital interaction as simple, fluid, intuitive, and efficient as possible. A great user experience will make your customers keep coming back for more!
