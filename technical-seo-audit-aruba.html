Professional SEO Website Audit

Online Visibility can make or break your Online Business

An audit is a standard procedure that should occur on a regular basis; it is essentially a "health check" for your website. A website audit is a process of assessing a website on various parameters that contribute to its search engine ranking and user experience. Netriches Aruba will scan your entire site and find where we can optimize and make technical changes to improve rankings.

Although you may feel that your website is well optimized, an SEO audit could reveal the one critical issue that’s holding your website back from doing even better. Once completed, the SEO audit will provide a definitive ‘To-Do’ list of how to increase your website rankings, but please keep in mind that search engines consistently update search algorithms that impact your website placement. If you want to identify issues with your website and improve your online presence through a detailed website audit, we can help.

What Type of SEO Audit do I need?

Commonly, an SEO audit refers to a "technical SEO audit". However, an audit can also be related to effective keyword targeting, content and PR, relevant backlinks, reputation management, or negative SEO, or for small local businesses, local SEO. Technical SEO audits focus on the technical aspect of your web infrastructure and often pivot to all other types of audits:

  • Content Audit: Assesses content quality and relevancy.
  • Link Audit: Looks at your external incoming backlinks.
  • Performance: Audit will check page loading and site speed.

WHAT DOES THE REPORT INCLUDE?

We will be looking at the following criteria:

User experience of your site

Is your business well-presented to visitors? Uncertainty leads to missed purchases, so we will identify any issues that could be turning your visitors off or not leading them the right way.

Your visibility on Google

"SEO stands for "search engine optimization." Is your website showing up where it should? We'll find any issues that are slowing down your ranking and make recommendations for how to boost traffic.

The speed and security

More than 40% of people leave when a website takes more than three seconds to load. Is your website performing well while keeping your users and data secure?

Netriches Aruba will email your SEO Audit Report within two business days, so you can see how your website scores across content quality, SEO, digital accessibility, and more. Below is a simplified version of an SEO audit checklist. While every website is different, and there are certainly more that can be included, this checklist provides a good understanding of where to begin.

  • Crawl analysis (errors)
  • Page load performance (speed)
  • Indexation (Accessibility)
  • Mobile performance
  • Site architecture
  • Keyword density
  • Backlinks analysis
  • Image optimization
  • Duplicate content
  • Redirects
  • Robots.txt
  • XML sitemap
