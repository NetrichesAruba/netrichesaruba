What Is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

Are you looking for SEO services in Aruba? Then you are in the right place. Search Engine Optimization, or in its shorter phrase, SEO, brings more customers to your business by ranking your website higher in search engines. Netriches Aruba Web Solutions provides a complete and high-quality SEO service. Having an SEO strategy is crucial for your website. Investing in effective SEO means making the right technical decisions.

Over time (whether small or large), that optimizes a website and maximizes its potential for search engine visibility. All of our web builds are designed with search in mind, so SEO is never an afterthought. Netriches Aruba SEO Services include the following:

SEO Website Audit

An SEO website audit is a standard procedure that should occur on a regular basis. A website audit will identify and provide solutions for your website’s main SEO issues. We provide you with a Website Audit report that will help you make changes to your website that have a huge impact on how it performs.

Keyword Planning

Keyword research is a key player in technical SEO and content marketing, making all kinds of content relevant and searchable to users. Knowing the right keywords to target is the heart of SEO effectiveness. Keywords are the essential meeting point between a searcher's query and your website.

On-page Optimization

On-page SEO is the practice of optimizing your website pages to get a higher ranking in the SERPs. This type of SEO focuses on features you can control on your website, including content, keyword research, content creation, header tag design and appearance, and other technical elements.

Image Optimization

Images are critical. Whether it is marketing banners, product images, or logos, it is impossible to imagine a website without images. SEO image optimization is the practice of making sure your images contain important information that explains what the image is about.

Local SEO

A Google My Business management service that gets your listing to the top of the search results. Local search engine optimization is the process of making your business or company visible for location-based searches. It showcases your website in search results to customers located near you at the exact moment when they need your services.

Schema Markup

Schema is code, added to your website’s html, that allows search engines to better understand what information is contained on the web page. By increasing the rich snippets that appear beneath each page title, schema markup helps to improve your website and the way each page is displayed in search engines.

Off-Page SEO

Common off-page SEO actions include building backlinks. For search engines that crawl the web, links are the streets between pages. Using sophisticated algorithms, the engines discover how pages are related to each other and in what ways. Your website moves up in search results when other relevant websites link back to your site.

Website Analytics Metrics
